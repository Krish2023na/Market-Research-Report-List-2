The second type is the Robotic Arm System, which uses robotic arms to precisely deposit construction materials based on digital designs. Lastly, the Others market includes different types of 3D printing systems, such as the contour crafting method that uses a nozzle to continuously deposit layers of building material. These types offer varying advantages and are used in diverse construction applications.</p></p>
