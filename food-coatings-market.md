<p><p>The food coatings market can be classified into two main types: dry and liquid coatings. Dry coatings refer to powders or flour-based mixtures that are applied to food surfaces to provide a crispy texture or enhance its appearance. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, involve the use of liquids such as oil, batter, or egg wash to coat food items, resulting in a moist texture or golden brown crust after cooking. Both types of coatings play a significant role in improving the overall sensory experience and presentation of various food products.</p></p>
