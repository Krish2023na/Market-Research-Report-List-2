<p><p>Video projectors are available in various market types, including DisplayPort, Dual HDMI, HDMI, MHL, USB Video, and Wireless. DisplayPort projectors support high-resolution digital audio and video transmission. Dual HDMI projectors enable the connection of two HDMI devices simultaneously. HDMI projectors provide a standard digital connection for audio and video transmission. MHL projectors allow for direct streaming and charging of mobile devices via a single cable. USB Video projectors enable direct playback of multimedia content from a USB device. Finally, Wireless projectors allow for seamless streaming of audio and video content from compatible devices without the need for cables.</p></p>
