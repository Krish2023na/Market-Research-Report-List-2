<p><p>Beam balances are a type of weighing scale commonly used in laboratories and educational settings. There are two main market types: double beam balances and triple beam balances. Double beam balances have two beams, allowing for more precise measurements with smaller increments. Triple beam balances have three beams, offering even greater accuracy and sensitivity. Both market types are used to measure the mass of objects by comparing the gravitational force on the object to the force on known masses. They are essential tools in scientific research and experimentation.</p></p>
