<p><p>The full body scanner market can be divided into two types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners use low-dose X-rays to create images of the human body, highlighting any hidden objects or substances. They are commonly used in airports and security checkpoints. On the other hand, millimeter wave scanners use non-ionizing electromagnetic waves to produce high-resolution images. They are preferred for their non-invasive nature and are used in applications such as medical imaging and security screening. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in ensuring safety and security in various settings.</p></p>
