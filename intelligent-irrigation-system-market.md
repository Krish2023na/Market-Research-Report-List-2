<p><ul><li>Weather-based Controllers</li><li>Sensor-based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intelligent Irrigation System market comprises two types of controllers: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers use real-time weather data to determine the irrigation schedule for a particular area. They factor in variables such as temperature, humidity, and precipitation to optimize water usage. On the other hand, sensor-based controllers utilize moisture sensors, soil moisture sensors, or other sensors to assess the actual irrigation needs of plants, ensuring water is used efficiently. These systems eliminate guesswork and efficiently irrigate based on weather conditions or plant requirements.</p></p>
