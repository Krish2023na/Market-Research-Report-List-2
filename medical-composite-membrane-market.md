<p><p>The medical composite membrane market consists of various types such as PSU (polysulfone) & PESU (polyethersulfone), PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride), PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), PP (polypropylene), modified acrylics, and others. PSU and PESU membranes offer excellent resistance to chemicals and high temperature, making them suitable for medical applications. PVDF membranes have high chemical resistance and durability. PTFE membranes are known for their nonstick properties, while PP membranes are cost-effective. Modified acrylic membranes are chemically resistant and provide good filtration. The "others" category includes various types of membranes that may possess different characteristics.</p></p>
