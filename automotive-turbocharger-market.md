<p><strong>The Automotive Turbocharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Turbocharging</li><li>Exhaust Gas Turbocharging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of automotive turbochargers: mechanical turbocharging and exhaust gas turbocharging. Mechanical turbocharging uses a mechanical device, such as a belt or chain, to drive the compressor and increase the air pressure in the intake manifold. This type of turbocharging is commonly used in smaller engines or applications where instant throttle response is required. On the other hand, exhaust gas turbocharging utilizes energy from the engine's exhaust gases to drive the turbine and compressor, resulting in higher power outputs. This type is more commonly found in larger engines or those requiring greater horsepower.</p></p>
