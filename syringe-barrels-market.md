<p><p>The syringe barrels market is categorized into three types: white syringe barrels, amber syringe barrels, and black syringe barrels. White syringe barrels are commonly used in medical and pharmaceutical applications as they allow easy visibility of the fluid inside. Amber syringe barrels, on the other hand, provide light protection and are suitable for light-sensitive fluids such as UV adhesives. Black syringe barrels are used when light exposure needs to be minimized, typically in applications involving photosensitive materials. Each type serves specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
