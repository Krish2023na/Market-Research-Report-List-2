<p><p>Wet macular degeneration, also known as neovascular AMD, is a chronic eye disease that affects the macula, the central part of the retina responsible for sharp, central vision. This condition occurs due to abnormal blood vessel growth beneath the retina, which causes leakage of blood and fluid into the macula, leading to vision loss.</p><p>The global wet macular degeneration market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend during the forecast period. Factors such as the rising geriatric population, increasing prevalence of age-related macular degeneration (AMD), and technological advancements in diagnostic and treatment options are driving market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the growing awareness about early diagnosis and treatment options, along with the availability of novel therapies, has further fueled market expansion. The introduction of anti-vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) drugs, such as Lucentis and Eylea, which are administered through intravitreal injections, have revolutionized the treatment landscape for wet AMD.</p><p>Moreover, ongoing research and development activities focusing on developing advanced drug delivery systems, gene therapies, and regenerative medicines are expected to propel market growth. The market's positive growth trajectory is also attributed to the rising healthcare expenditure, improving healthcare infrastructure, and increasing adoption of advanced healthcare technologies in developing economies.</p><p>Despite the market's growth potential, certain challenges hinder its progress. High treatment costs, limited reimbursement policies, and potential side effects associated with the available therapies are some of the factors that may restrain market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, the wet macular degeneration market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors such as an aging population, advances in treatments, and increased awareness. However, challenges related to cost and reimbursement policies need to be addressed to ensure sustainable market expansion.
