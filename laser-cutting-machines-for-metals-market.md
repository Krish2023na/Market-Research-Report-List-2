<p><p>The laser cutting machines market for metals can be divided into three types: CO2 laser cutting machines, Fiber laser cutting machines, and YAG cutting machines. CO2 laser cutting machines use a gas mixture to generate high-energy laser beams for cutting metal. Fiber laser cutting machines use optical fiber to deliver laser beams, making them more efficient and precise. YAG cutting machines employ yttrium-aluminum-garnet crystals to generate laser beams. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different metal cutting applications based on factors like speed, precision, and material thickness.</p></p>
