<p><strong>The Glaucoma Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prostaglandins Analogues (PGAs)</li><li>Non PGAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma pharmaceuticals market is categorized into two types: Prostaglandins Analogues (PGAs) and Non PGAs. PGAs are a type of medication that lower intraocular pressure by increasing the drainage of fluid from the eye. They are the most commonly prescribed medication for glaucoma due to their high effectiveness and few side effects. On the other hand, non PGAs encompass various drugs that also help reduce intraocular pressure but utilize different mechanisms of action, such as inhibiting the production of fluid or improving its outflow through different pathways.</p></p>
