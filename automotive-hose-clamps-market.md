<p><strong>The Automotive Hose Clamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw/Band (Worm Gear) Clamps</li><li>Spring Clamps</li><li>Wire Clamps</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hose clamps market includes various types of clamps used for securing hoses in vehicles. Screw/Band clamps, also known as worm gear clamps, are the most common type. They consist of a metal band tightened with a screw, offering adjustable and secure fitting. Spring clamps feature a spring-like design, providing constant tension. Wire clamps use a thin wire wrapped around the hose and tightened with pliers. Other types of clamps may include pinch clamps, ear clamps, and T-bolt clamps, each with their unique mechanism for securing automotive hoses.</p></p>
