<p><p>Fragile X Syndrome Genetic Testing is a type of genetic testing that helps in diagnosing and assessing the risk of Fragile X Syndrome, a genetic disorder. There are different types of markets for this testing. Genetic Screening involves analyzing DNA to identify individuals at risk of having the syndrome. Reproductive Genetic Testing is performed on couples planning to have children, aiming to identify if they carry the Fragile X gene. Diagnostic Test confirms the presence of the syndrome in individuals showing symptoms. Gene Carrier Test determines if individuals carry the gene without having any symptoms. Testing Before Symptoms Appear helps in detecting the syndrome in individuals who have a family history or are at risk of developing symptoms in the future.</p></p>
