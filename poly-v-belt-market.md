<p><p>A poly V belt, also known as a multi-rib belt or serpentine belt, is a power transmission belt that is used in various industries for efficient and reliable power transfer. It consists of multiple V-shaped ribs that run longitudinally on the belt's inner side, which make contact with corresponding grooves on the pulleys. This design ensures increased contact area and better power transmission efficiency compared to traditional flat belts.</p><p>The poly V belt market has been experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue its positive trend in the coming years. The key drivers for this growth include the increasing adoption of automated manufacturing processes, the rising demand for energy-efficient systems, and the growing automotive industry.
