<p><p>The acne medication market is divided into two main types: topical medication and oral medication. Topical medication refers to creams, gels, or lotions that are directly applied to the affected areas of the skin. They work by reducing oil production, eliminating bacteria, and unclogging pores. On the other hand, oral medication involves the consumption of pills or capsules that target acne from within the body. They often contain hormones or antibiotics to regulate oil production and kill bacteria that cause acne. Both types play a crucial role in treating and managing acne.</p></p>
