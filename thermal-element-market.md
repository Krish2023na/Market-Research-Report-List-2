<p><p>The thermal element market types include positive temperature and negative temperature elements. Positive temperature elements consist of materials that increase resistance as temperature rises, allowing them to measure and control heat levels in applications such as heating systems and thermostats. On the other hand, negative temperature elements are made of materials that decrease resistance as temperature increases. These elements find use in applications like cooling systems and temperature controllers. Both types of thermal elements play a crucial role in maintaining controlled and efficient temperature management in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The thermal element market includes three key applications: thermal resistance, thermocouple, and thermistor. Thermal resistance is used to measure the amount of heat flowing through a material and is commonly employed in HVAC systems. Thermocouples are temperature sensors composed of two wires of different metals, which generate an electromotive force proportional to the temperature difference and are widely used in industrial processes and temperature monitoring. Thermistors are temperature-sensitive resistors offering a high level of accuracy and sensitivity, commonly utilized in medical devices, automotive applications, and consumer electronics for temperature measurement and control.</p></p>
