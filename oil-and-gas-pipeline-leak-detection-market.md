<p><p>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection market offers various types of technologies to detect and prevent leaks. Mass-Volume Balance method uses pressure and flow rate measurements to identify discrepancies indicating a possible leakage. Acoustic/Ultrasonic systems detect leaks by analyzing sound patterns produced by escaping fluids. Fiber Optics technology uses optical fibers to monitor changes in temperature or pressure caused by leaks. Vapor Sensing systems utilize sensors to identify hydrocarbon vapor concentrations in the air, indicating a potential pipeline leak.</p></p>
