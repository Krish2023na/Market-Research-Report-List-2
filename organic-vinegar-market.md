<p><p>The organic vinegar market can be divided into various types. Mature vinegar refers to vinegar that has been aged for a longer period, resulting in a more intense flavor. Balsamic vinegar is known for its rich, sweet and tangy taste, often used in salad dressings. White vinegar is clear and has a sharp, acidic taste, commonly used for cleaning purposes. Wine vinegar is made from fermented wine and adds a subtle fruity flavor to dishes. Cidar vinegar is made from fermented apples and has a slightly sweet and fruity taste.
