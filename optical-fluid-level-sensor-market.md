<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fluid level sensor market consists of two main types: contact and contactless sensors. Contact sensors make direct physical contact with the liquid to measure its level. They utilize technologies such as capacitive or resistive sensing. On the other hand, contactless sensors do not require physical contact with the liquid. They use optical or ultrasonic technologies to measure the fluid level. Both types have their advantages and applications based on the specific needs of industries such as automotive, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
