<p><p>The interactive display market encompasses two main types: LCD and LED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) panels use liquid crystals to create images and are widely used in various devices due to their energy efficiency and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays utilize light-emitting diodes to produce images, offering better brightness and color accuracy. LED displays are commonly used in large-scale applications such as outdoor advertising or signage. Both LCD and LED displays play a significant role in the interactive display market, serving diverse needs across industries.</p></p>
