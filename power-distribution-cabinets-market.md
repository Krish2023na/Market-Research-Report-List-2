The market for power distribution cabinets can be divided into three types based on voltage levels: low-voltage, middle-voltage, and high-voltage. </p><p>Low-voltage power distribution cabinets are typically used in residential, commercial, and small-scale industrial settings, where the voltage level is less than 1,000 volts. Middle-voltage power distribution cabinets are employed in larger industries and infrastructure projects, where the voltage level ranges between 1,000 and 35,000 volts. High-voltage power distribution cabinets are utilized in utility and grid systems, where the voltage level exceeds 35,000 volts. These classifications help cater to different power distribution requirements in a safe and efficient manner.</p></p>
