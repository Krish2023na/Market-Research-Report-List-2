<p><ul><li>Freestanding</li><li>Wall Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency shower market consists of two main types: freestanding and wall mounted showers. Freestanding showers are stand-alone units that can be placed anywhere in a facility and are convenient for areas with limited wall space. They usually have a self-supporting structure with a large overhead showerhead. On the other hand, wall mounted showers are fixed to a wall and are suitable for areas with ample wall space. They have a compact design and often feature an eyewash unit alongside the showerhead for combined emergency response capabilities.</p></p>
