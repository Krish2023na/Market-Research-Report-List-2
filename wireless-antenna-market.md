<p><strong>The Wireless Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UHF</li><li>VHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless antenna market encompasses various types, including UHF (Ultra High Frequency) and VHF (Very High Frequency) antennas. UHF antennas operate in the frequency range of 300 MHz to 3 GHz, while VHF antennas operate in the range of 30 MHz to 300 MHz. UHF antennas are commonly used for communications applications, such as mobile phones, wireless routers, and TV broadcasting. On the other hand, VHF antennas find their applications in mediums like AM/FM radio broadcasting, analog television broadcasting, and marine communications. Both UHF and VHF antennas play an essential role in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
