<p><strong>The Portable TDS Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable TDS Meters market consists of three main types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. Single Channel meters are designed to measure the total dissolved solids (TDS) in a specific sample with one channel only. Dual Channel meters, as the name suggests, have two channels, allowing for simultaneous measurements of TDS in two different samples. Lastly, Multi Channel meters offer the capability to measure TDS in multiple samples simultaneously, thereby increasing efficiency and throughput in various applications.</p></p>
