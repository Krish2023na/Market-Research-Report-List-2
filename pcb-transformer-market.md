<p><p>The PCB Transformer market consists of different types, namely dual primary, dual secondary, single primary, dual secondary, and single primary, single secondary. Dual primary transformers have two input windings to handle two different primary voltages. Dual secondary transformers, on the other hand, have two output windings to deliver two different secondary voltages. Single primary, dual secondary transformers have one input winding and two output windings, while single primary, single secondary transformers have one input winding and one output winding. These variations cater to different voltage requirements and application scenarios in various electronic devices.</p></p>
