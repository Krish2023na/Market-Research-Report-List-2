<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Uniform particle size ion exchange resins are categorized into four types: strong acid, weak acid, strong base, and weak base. Strong acid resins have the highest capacity for cation exchange and are used for removing positively charged ions from water. Weak acid resins have a lower capacity, and they remove both positively charged and uncharged particles. Strong base resins are highly effective for anion exchange, removing negatively charged ions. Weak base resins have a lower capacity for anion exchange and remove negatively charged and uncharged particles. These resins are widely used in water treatment and purification processes.</p></p>
