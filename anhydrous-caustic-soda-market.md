<p><p>Anhydrous caustic soda is available in two main market types: caustic soda flake and caustic soda particle. Caustic soda flake is produced by the evaporation of caustic soda solution, resulting in the formation of solid flakes. On the other hand, caustic soda particle is produced by grinding the solid flakes into a fine powder. Both forms are highly corrosive and are commonly used in various industries such as chemical manufacturing, water treatment, and cleaning products. The choice between flake and particle depends on specific application requirements.</p></p>
