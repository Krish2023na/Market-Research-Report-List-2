<p><p>Diffusion pumps are used in various industries to create vacuum conditions. Two common types of diffusion pumps in the market are Silicone Oil diffusion pumps and Polyphenyl Ethers diffusion pumps. Silicone Oil diffusion pumps use silicone oil as the working fluid, which vaporizes and carries gas molecules out of the system. On the other hand, Polyphenyl Ethers diffusion pumps use polyphenyl ethers as the working fluid, providing similar vacuum capabilities. These different types of diffusion pumps serve the same purpose of enabling efficient vacuum conditions in different industrial applications.</p></p>
