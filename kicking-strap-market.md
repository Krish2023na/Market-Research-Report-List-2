<p><p>The kicking strap market offers different types of devices that are used to control the movement of a boat's boom. Hydraulic kicking straps utilize hydraulic systems to provide adjustable tension and control. Rigid kicking straps are fixed in position and provide a set level of tension. Reverse thrust kicking straps add a reverse thrust feature, allowing the boom to move in the opposite direction. Pneumatic kicking straps use compressed air or gas to provide tension and control. These various types provide boaters with options to suit their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
