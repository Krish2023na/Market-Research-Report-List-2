<p><p>Medium viscosity bone cement is a medical material used in orthopedic surgeries, specifically in joint replacement procedures. It is a bio-compatible substance that is injected into the bone cavities to anchor prosthetic implants, ensuring stability and long-lasting fixation. It possesses intermediate flow characteristics between low and high viscosity bone cement, making it suitable for a wide range of orthopedic applications.</p><p>The medium viscosity bone cement market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers of this growth is the increasing prevalence of orthopedic disorders, particularly among the elderly population. As the population ages, the demand for joint replacement surgeries is anticipated to rise, subsequently boosting the demand for medium viscosity bone cement.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in cement technology and the introduction of innovative products supporting minimally invasive procedures have further propelled market growth. Surgeons are increasingly using medium viscosity bone cement due to its ability to fill the gaps between the bone and the implant, reducing the risk of implant loosening and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing various trends, including the adoption of antibiotic-loaded bone cement to prevent infection during surgeries and the growing preference for bone cement with high radiopacity, facilitating accurate visualization during implant placement.</p><p>In conclusion, the medium viscosity bone cement market is expected to grow at a steady pace, with a projected CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period.
