<p><p>Folding screen mobile phones are divided into two market types: inner fold and out fold. Inner fold phones have a screen that folds inwardly, protecting it from external elements when closed. This design offers durability and a more compact form factor, making it suitable for everyday use. On the other hand, out fold phones have a screen that folds outwardly. This design provides a larger display when opened, resembling a tablet-like experience. Out fold phones are often favored for multimedia and gaming purposes due to their immersive viewing experience.  Overall, these market types cater to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
