<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Impact Hammer</li><li>Electric Impact Hammer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impact hammers market consists of two main types: hydraulic impact hammers and electric impact hammers. Hydraulic impact hammers utilize hydraulic power to generate the necessary force to drive and install piles or perform other heavy-duty tasks. They offer high impact energy and are suitable for large-scale projects. On the other hand, electric impact hammers are powered by electricity and are typically smaller and more lightweight than hydraulic ones. They are commonly used for lighter applications such as demolition work or driving smaller piles.</p></p>
