<p><p>External power supplies for LED driving refer to power supplies that are separate from the LED lighting fixture. These power supplies are connected externally to the LED fixture and provide the necessary electrical voltage and current for their operation. On the other hand, built-in power supplies are incorporated within the LED lighting fixture itself. This means that the power supply is integrated into the design of the fixture, eliminating the need for external connections. Both types serve the purpose of providing power to LED lighting systems but differ in terms of their physical arrangement.</p></p>
