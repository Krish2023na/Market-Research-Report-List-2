<p><p>FBG Sensor, also known as Fiber Bragg Grating Sensor, is an optical sensing technology used for measuring various physical quantities, such as strain, pressure, temperature, and acceleration. It consists of an optical fiber with a periodic variation in its refractive index, which causes certain wavelengths of light to be reflected back while allowing others to pass through. These reflected wavelengths provide information about the physical quantities being measured.</p><p>The current outlook of the FBG Sensor market is quite positive. <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>FBG sensor market is divided into two types: point FBG and distributed FBG. Point FBG sensors consist of a single sensor element and are used for measuring a specific point. These sensors are cost-effective and commonly used in civil engineering, aerospace, and energy industries. On the other hand, distributed FBG sensors use multiple sensor elements along the fiber length, allowing for continuous measurements. These sensors are mostly utilized in structural health monitoring, oil and gas pipelines, and temperature monitoring applications due to their high sensitivity and accuracy.</p></p>
