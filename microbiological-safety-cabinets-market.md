<p><p>Microbiological Safety Cabinets (MSCs) are specialized equipment used to provide a high level of protection for laboratory personnel and the environment when working with biological agents. Class II MSCs are the most common type, providing personnel, environmental, and product protection through the use of HEPA filtered exhaust air. Class I MSCs offer personnel and environmental protection, but not product protection. Class III MSCs are entirely gas-tight and provide the highest level of protection by completely isolating the sample and operator from the environment.</p></p>
