<p><p>An electromagnetic contactor is an electrical device used to switch an electrical circuit on/off. It consists of a coil and a set of contacts that open and close to control the flow of electricity. When the coil is energized, it generates a magnetic field that attracts the contacts, closing the circuit. When the coil is de-energized, the contacts open, interrupting the circuit.</p><p>The electromagnetic contactor market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors.
