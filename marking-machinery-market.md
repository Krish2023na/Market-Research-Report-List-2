<p><ul><li>Laser Marking Machinery</li><li>Mechanical Marking Machinery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marking machinery refers to the equipment used to create permanent marks on various materials. Laser marking machinery utilizes lasers to etch or engrave the mark onto the surface, offering precision and versatility. On the other hand, mechanical marking machinery employs physical tools like stylus or dies to stamp or indent the mark onto the material, providing durability and cost-effectiveness. Both types of machinery cater to different industrial needs, such as product identification, traceability, or personalization.</p></p>
