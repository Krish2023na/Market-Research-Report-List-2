<p><p>The drain snake market consists of two primary types: manual drain snakes and electric drain snakes. Manual drain snakes are handheld tools used for unclogging pipes and drains. They require physical effort from the user to operate and typically have a coiled metal wire attached to a handle. Electric drain snakes, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and eliminate the need for manual labor. They usually consist of a motorized unit with a rotating cable to remove blockages. Both types serve the purpose of clearing drains, but differ in terms of operation and power source.</p></p>
