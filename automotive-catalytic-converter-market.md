<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter market can be categorized into various types. Two-way converters are designed to reduce carbon monoxide (CO) and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) emissions from the engine's exhaust system. Three-way converters, on the other hand, not only reduce CO and HC emissions but also convert nitrogen oxides (NOx) into harmless gases. Apart from these, there are other types of converters available in the market, including four-way converters and diesel oxidation catalysts, which are specifically designed for diesel engines. These converters play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
