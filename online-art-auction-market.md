<p><ul><li>Reserved Auction</li><li>Unreserved Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Online art auction markets can be categorized into two types: reserved auction and unreserved auction. In a reserved auction, the seller sets a confidential minimum price for the artwork, and if the bidding does not reach this minimum, the artwork remains unsold. In contrast, an unreserved auction has no minimum price set, ensuring the artwork will be sold regardless of the bidding outcome. Both types provide opportunities for buyers and sellers in the online art market, allowing for different pricing strategies and outcomes based on the selling preferences of the individuals involved.</p></p>
