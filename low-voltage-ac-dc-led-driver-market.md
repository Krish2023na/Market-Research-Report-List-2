<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The low voltage AC-DC LED driver market consists of three main types: buck drivers, boost drivers, and multi-channel drivers. Buck drivers are used to lower the voltage input to the LED, resulting in a constant current output. Boost drivers, on the other hand, increase the voltage input to provide a constant current output. Lastly, multi-channel drivers enable the control of multiple LED channels independently, allowing for different colors or brightness levels. These drivers play a crucial role in powering LED lighting systems with efficient and reliable performance.</p></p>
