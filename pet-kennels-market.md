This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing number of pet owners, coupled with rising disposable incomes, has led to a higher demand for pet care services. As pet owners are becoming more conscious about their pets' well-being and safety, they are opting for kennels that offer quality care and amenities.</p><p>Additionally, the growing trend of pet humanization has also contributed to the market's growth. Pet owners are increasingly treating their pets as family members and are therefore willing to spend on premium services that ensure their pets' comfort and happiness. This has led kennels to upgrade their facilities and services to cater to these demanding pet owners.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has also had a positive impact on the pet kennels market. With travel restrictions and lockdown measures in place, many pet owners have been unable to travel with their pets. However, challenges such as increased competition and stringent regulations regarding animal welfare may impact the market's growth to some extent. Overall, the pet kennels market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years due to the increasing pet ownership and changing attitudes towards pet care.</p></p>
