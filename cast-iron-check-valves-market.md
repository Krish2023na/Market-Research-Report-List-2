<p><p>The Cast Iron Check Valves market can be categorized into two types based on their connection methods: Flanged Connection and Threaded Connection. </p><p>Flanged Connection check valves are equipped with flanges that allow easy installation and removal by bolting them between two pipe flanges. They provide a secure and tight connection, minimizing leakage risks. </p><p>On the other hand, Threaded Connection check valves have threaded ends that can be screwed onto the corresponding threaded pipes. This type of connection offers convenience and flexibility for installation in smaller pipe systems.</p></p>
