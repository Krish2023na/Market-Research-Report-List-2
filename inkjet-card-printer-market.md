<p><p>The inkjet card printer market consists of various types of printing technologies. Thermal foaming is a type of inkjet printing that uses heat to create bubbles in the ink, which then burst onto the card surface. This technology provides fast printing speed and high quality. Micro Piezo, on the other hand, uses tiny electrically charged nozzles that carefully control the drop size and placement of the ink on the card. This method ensures precise printing and allows for a wider range of colors and details.</p></p>
