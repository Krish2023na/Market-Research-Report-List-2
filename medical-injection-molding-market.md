<p><strong>The Medical Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Runner</li><li>Hot Runner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical injection molding is a technique used to produce medical devices and components. Cold runner and hot runner are two types of molds used in this market. In cold runner systems, the molten material is injected into the mold through a channel, which needs to be manually removed after cooling. On the other hand, hot runner systems maintain the material in a molten state, reducing waste and improving efficiency. Both types have their advantages, and the choice depends on the specific requirements of the medical injection molding process.</p></p>
