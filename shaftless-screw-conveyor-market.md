<p><p>A shaftless screw conveyor is a type of conveyor system used to transport materials horizontally or at a slight incline without the need for a central shaft. It consists of a helical screw blade, housed within a trough, which rotates to move materials from one end to the other. This design eliminates the need for a central shaft, resulting in a compact and more versatile conveyor system.</p><p>The future outlook of the shaftless screw conveyor market is promising, with a projected growth rate of 11.5% during the forecasted period. This growth can be attributed to various factors such as increased demand for efficient material handling systems, advancements in automation technologies, and stringent regulations related to worker safety and environmental protection.</p><p>Currently, the shaftless screw conveyor market is driven by the growing need for bulk material handling in industries such as food processing, wastewater treatment, mining, and agriculture. The ability of shaftless screw conveyors to handle a wide range of materials, including sticky and fibrous substances, makes them highly desirable in these industries.</p><p>The market forecast for shaftless screw conveyors indicates substantial growth opportunities in emerging economies due to rapid industrialization and infrastructure development.
