<p><p>The LED light curable adhesive market consists of three main types: low viscosity, high viscosity, and others. Low viscosity adhesives have a thinner consistency and are suitable for applications that require quick and easy flow into small gaps or bonding delicate components. High viscosity adhesives, on the other hand, have a thicker consistency and are used for applications that require strong structural bonding or filling gaps between larger components.
