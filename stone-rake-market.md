<p><p>Stone rakes refer to equipment used for removing stones and debris from soil or surfaces. There are two main types of stone rakes in the market: rotary stone rake and rigid stone rake. The rotary stone rake uses rotating tines or blades to gather and collect stones, making it suitable for larger areas or heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, a rigid stone rake consists of fixed blades that are manually operated, making it more suitable for smaller areas or lighter tasks. Both types of stone rakes are commonly used in agriculture, construction, and landscaping industries.</p></p>
