<p><ul><li>Back Pressure Indicator</li><li>Differential Pressure Indicator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blockage indicators are devices used in various industries to monitor and indicate blockages or restrictions in a system. There are different types of blockage indicators available in the market. Back pressure indicators measure the pressure created due to a blockage, providing insight into the severity of the obstruction. Differential pressure indicators compare the pressure on either side of a potential blockage, allowing for identification of blockage location. Others include visual indicators and electronic sensors that detect the presence of blockages. These indicators help maintain system efficiency and prevent damage or failures.</p></p>
