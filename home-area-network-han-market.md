<p><p>A Home Area Network (HAN) is a communication network within a residential property that connects various devices and appliances together. ZigBee, HomePlug, Wireless M-Bus, and others are market types that provide different technologies for HANs. ZigBee utilizes low-power wireless technology, enabling devices to communicate wirelessly with each other. HomePlug uses power lines to transmit data and connect devices throughout the home. Wireless M-Bus is a wireless communication standard specifically designed for metering applications. Other market types may include alternative technologies like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth that also enable connectivity within a HAN.</p></p>
