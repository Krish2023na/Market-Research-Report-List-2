<p><p>The small business phone platform market consists of various types of solutions. PBX (Private Branch Exchange) allows internal communication within a company. Auto attendant directs calls using pre-recorded messages. IVR (Interactive Voice Response) system responds to caller input. CTI (Computer Telephony Integration) integrates telephony with computer systems. FMFM (Find Me/Follow Me) enables call forwarding to multiple devices. Softphone allows phone calls through software on computers or smartphones. Voicemail-to-email converts voicemails to email messages. Conferencing enables multiple participants to join calls or video meetings. These solutions aim to enhance communication efficiency for small businesses.</p></p>
