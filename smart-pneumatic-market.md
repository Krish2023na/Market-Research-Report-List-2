<p><p>Smart pneumatic market types include valves, actuators, and modules. Valves are devices that control the flow of gas or fluid in a system, while actuators convert energy into mechanical motion for controlling valves or other mechanisms. Modules are comprehensive systems that integrate valves and actuators with additional functionalities like communication capabilities, sensing, or data processing. These smart pneumatic components are equipped with advanced technologies such as sensors, controllers, and communication protocols to provide efficient and automated control in industrial applications, improving productivity, accuracy, and operational safety.</p></p>
