<p><p>Hematology treatment market consists of two main types: Over-the-Counter (OTC) drugs and Prescription (Rx) drugs market. OTC drugs refer to medications that can be purchased without a prescription, typically used for common hematologic conditions such as iron deficiency anemia. These drugs are easily accessible to consumers at pharmacies or supermarkets. On the other hand, Rx drugs require a prescription from a healthcare professional and are used for more complex or serious hematological disorders, such as leukemia or blood clotting disorders. These drugs are dispensed by licensed pharmacies.</p></p>
