<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Flowable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hemostat market includes two types: Mechanical Type and Flowable Type. Mechanical Type hemostats are used to physically block blood flow by clamping or sealing blood vessels. These are often made of metal and require manual application. On the other hand, Flowable Type hemostats are liquid or gel-like substances that are applied directly to the bleeding site. They work by promoting coagulation and forming a clot to stop bleeding. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient or procedure.</p></p>
