<p><p>The veterinary cardiac monitoring devices market includes various types of devices used for monitoring the heart of animals. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record the heart's activity for a certain period, usually 24 to 48 hours. Event recorders are used to record cardiac events when triggered by the animal's symptoms or by a veterinarian. Other types of monitoring devices include implantable loop recorders and remote cardiac monitors, which allow for long-term monitoring and remote data transfer. These devices aid in the diagnosis and management of cardiac conditions in animals.</p></p>
