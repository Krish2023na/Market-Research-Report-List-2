<p><strong>The Industrial Large Tank Mixer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top mounted</li><li>Side mounted</li><li>Bottom mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial large tank mixer market consists of three primary types: top mounted, side mounted, and bottom mounted mixers. Top mounted mixers are connected to the top of the tank and provide mixing action from above. Side mounted mixers are installed on the side of the tank and use impellers or paddles to mix the contents. Bottom mounted mixers are attached to the bottom of the tank and use propellers or impellers to mix the liquid from below. These different types allow for flexibility in meeting the mixing requirements of various industrial applications.</p></p>
