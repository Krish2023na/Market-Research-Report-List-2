<p><p>The heating pad market can be broadly categorized into three types: microwavable, electric, and chemical heating pads. Microwavable heating pads are designed to be heated in a microwave before use, providing warmth and comfort. Electric heating pads, on the other hand, use electricity to generate heat and usually come with adjustable temperature settings. Lastly, chemical heating pads utilize chemical reactions to produce heat when activated. Each type offers its own advantages and caters to different preferences and needs of consumers seeking therapeutic and soothing relief.</p></p>
