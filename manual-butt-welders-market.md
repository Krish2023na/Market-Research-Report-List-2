<p><p>Manual butt welders can be classified into two main types: handheld and desktop welders. Handheld butt welders are designed to be portable and are operated by hand, making them suitable for on-the-go welding tasks. They offer flexibility and ease of use, allowing users to create precise welds in various positions. On the other hand, desktop butt welders are larger and are intended for stationary use. They provide stability and can accommodate thicker materials, making them ideal for heavy-duty welding applications. Both types serve different purposes and cater to specific needs in the manual butt welding market.</p></p>
