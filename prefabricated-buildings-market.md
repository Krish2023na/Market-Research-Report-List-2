<p><strong>The Prefabricated Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beam and Column System</li><li>Floor and Roof System</li><li>Bearing Wall System</li><li>Facade System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prefabricated Buildings Market Types include the following systems:</p><p>1. Beam and Column System: This type of system uses vertical and horizontal beams and columns connected together to create a rigid framework for the building structure.</p><p>2. Floor and Roof System: This system involves the precast concrete or steel components that form the floors and roofs of the building, which are assembled and installed on-site.</p><p>3. Bearing Wall System: In this system, load-bearing walls are manufactured off-site and then transported and assembled at the construction site, providing structural support for the entire building.</p><p>4. Facade System: This system includes the exterior walls of the building, which are typically prefabricated and designed to enhance the aesthetics and energy efficiency of the structure.</p><p>These different systems in the prefabricated buildings market offer various advantages such as speed of construction, cost-effectiveness, design flexibility, and sustainability.</p></p>
