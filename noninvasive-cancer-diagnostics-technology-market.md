<p><p>Noninvasive cancer diagnostic technology includes various types of tests used for detecting cancer without the need for invasive procedures. Immunoassays are tests that detect cancer-specific markers in body fluids or tissues using antibodies. Molecular diagnostics involve analyzing genetic material to identify cancer-related mutations or changes. Clinical chemistry tests analyze blood or urine samples for abnormal levels of substances indicating cancer. Other noninvasive cancer diagnostics may include imaging techniques like MRI or CT scans. These technologies help in early detection and monitoring of cancer, aiding in timely treatment decisions.</p></p>
