<p><strong>The Explosion-proof Electric Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EXd Type</li><li>Increased-Safety Type</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are various types of explosion-proof electric motors available in the market. The EXd type is designed to contain any internal explosion within the motor, preventing it from igniting the surrounding atmosphere. Increased-Safety type motors are built with added safety features, like restricted openings, to prevent sparks or arcs from escaping. Other types include encapsulated motors, which are completely enclosed to prevent any hazardous substances from entering, and pressurized motors that maintain a higher internal pressure than the surrounding atmosphere, preventing any explosive gases from entering.</p></p>
