<p><p>Hip protectors are devices designed to prevent hip fractures by absorbing and redistributing impact forces in case of a fall. Soft hip protectors are typically made of foam or fabric and provide cushioning and impact absorption. On the other hand, hard hip protectors are typically made of rigid plastics or metals and provide a protective shell to prevent hip fractures. Both types of hip protectors aim to reduce the risk of hip injuries and enhance safety for individuals at risk of falling.</p></p>
