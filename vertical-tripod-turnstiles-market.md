<p><p>The vertical tripod turnstiles market consists of two main types: automatic and mechanical turnstiles. Automatic turnstiles are equipped with sensors and electromechanical systems to efficiently control pedestrian traffic flow. These turnstiles are often used in high-security areas and require minimal manual intervention. On the other hand, mechanical turnstiles operate manually and rely on physical barriers or rotating arms to control access. These turnstiles are commonly found in areas where security measures are less stringent or for temporary crowd control purposes.</p></p>
