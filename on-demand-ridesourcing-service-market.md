<p><p>On-demand ridesourcing service market types include carpooling, carsharing, and e-taxi. Carsharing allows users to rent a vehicle for a short period, sharing the costs and avoiding the need for individual ownership. E-taxi refers to the on-demand taxi services provided through smartphone applications, which enable users to request a ride with professional drivers. These market types all aim to provide convenient transportation options, reduce vehicle ownership, and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
