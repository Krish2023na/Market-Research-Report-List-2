<p><p>The hand applicator market consists of different types, namely, hard board and soft pad. Hard boards are rigid surfaces often made of materials like plastic or wood. They provide a sturdy base for applying products onto surfaces. Soft pads, on the other hand, are flexible and cushioned surfaces typically made of sponge or foam. Both hard board and soft pad hand applicators serve as essential tools for various purposes, such as applying paint or cleaning substances.</p></p>
