<p><p>Managed hybrid cloud hosting refers to a hosting solution that combines both cloud-based infrastructure and on-premises servers. The cloud-based market type involves utilizing remote servers to store and manage data, applications, and processes, offering scalability and accessibility benefits. On the other hand, the on-premises market type involves deploying infrastructure within the organization's premises, giving full control and customization but lacking the scalability of the cloud. Managed hybrid cloud hosting combines the advantages of both models, allowing organizations to have the flexibility and scalability of the cloud while retaining control over certain critical data and applications on-premises.</p></p>
