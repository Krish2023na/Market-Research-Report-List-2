<p><ul><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apheresis equipment is utilized for the separation and collection of specific blood components. The two main types of apheresis equipment are centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation involves the use of a rotating device to separate blood components based on their density, while membrane filtration uses porous filters to selectively remove certain components from the blood. Both methods are critical in various medical procedures, such as blood donation, plasma exchange, and stem cell transplantation, for effectively collecting specific components while returning the remainder of the blood to the patient.</p></p>
