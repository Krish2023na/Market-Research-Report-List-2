<p><p>The smartphone audio decoder market comprises two main types: Lossless Compression and Lossy Compression. Lossless Compression technology efficiently reduces the size of audio files without sacrificing their quality, ensuring an exact replication of the original sound. Lossy Compression, on the other hand, achieves higher compression ratios by discarding some audio data, resulting in a slight loss of quality. Both types have their advantages and are designed to cater to different user preferences and requirements in terms of audio quality and file size on smartphones.</p></p>
