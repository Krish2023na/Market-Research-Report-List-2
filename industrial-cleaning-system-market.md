<p><p>The industrial cleaning system market can be categorized into two main types: wet cleaning and dry cleaning. Wet cleaning involves the use of water and cleaning agents to remove dirt, stains, and contaminants from surfaces and equipment. It is widely used in industries where water can be easily accessed and does not cause any damage. On the other hand, dry cleaning uses specialized machines and solvents to clean surfaces without the need for water. It is suitable for industries where water-based cleaning is not feasible or could lead to damage.</p></p>
