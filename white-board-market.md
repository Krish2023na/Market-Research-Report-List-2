Melamine whiteboards are the most common and affordable option, made with a melamine-coated particle board. Porcelain whiteboards are more durable and scratch-resistant, using a ceramic surface fused to a steel backing. Glass whiteboards have a sleek and modern appearance, made with tempered glass that is resistant to stains and ghosting. Each type offers different features and price ranges to cater to the specific needs and preferences of users.</p></p>
