<p><ul><li>Sterile Absorbable Surgical Yarns</li><li>Sterile Nonabsorbable Surgical Yarns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile surgical yarn market consists of two types: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Sterile absorbable surgical yarns are made from materials that can be broken down and absorbed by the body over time, such as natural materials or synthetic polymers. These yarns are commonly used in surgical procedures where long-term suture integrity is not required. On the other hand, sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns are made from materials that cannot be absorbed by the body and are designed for long-term wound closure and support.</p></p>
