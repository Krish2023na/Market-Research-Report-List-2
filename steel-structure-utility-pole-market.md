<p><p>A steel structure utility pole, also known as a transmission tower or electric pole, is a tall structure typically made of steel that supports overhead power lines for the transmission of electricity from power plants to various distribution points. First, the increasing demand for electricity in both developed and developing countries is driving the need for a well-developed power transmission and distribution infrastructure. Additionally, the ongoing upgrades and replacements of aging transmission infrastructure are further supporting market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing focus on renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, is also contributing to the demand for steel structure utility poles. These poles are essential for connecting renewable energy generation facilities to the existing electrical grid.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a shift towards the installation of advanced and smart steel structure utility poles.
