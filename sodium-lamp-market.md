<p><p>The Sodium Lamp market can be categorised into two main types: High Pressure Sodium Lamp and Low Pressure Sodium Lamp. High Pressure Sodium Lamps are widely used for outdoor lighting purposes due to their efficiency and long lifespan. They emit a warm golden color and are commonly used for street lighting.
