<p><p>The Veterinary CT Systems Market includes two main types: Multi Slice CT Scanner and Portable CT Scanner. Multi Slice CT Scanners use advanced technology to capture multiple images of animals' body parts simultaneously, allowing for detailed and precise diagnosis. These scanners are usually installed in veterinary hospitals or clinics. On the other hand, Portable CT Scanners are compact and lightweight, designed for mobile use or in-field diagnosis.
