<p><p>The Vehicle Refinish Coating market primarily consists of four types of coatings: Primer, Base Coat, Top Coat, and Clear Coat. Primer is the initial layer applied to the vehicle surface to enhance adhesion and provide corrosion protection. Base Coat is the colored layer that gives the vehicle its desired appearance. Top Coat is a transparent layer that adds gloss and durability to the finish. Clear Coat, as the name suggests, is a protective layer that provides resistance against UV rays, chemicals, and scratches, while also enhancing the shine of the vehicle. These coatings work together to create a high-quality and long-lasting finish for vehicles.</p></p>
