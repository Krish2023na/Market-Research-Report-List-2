<p><p>The ePTFE Vascular Graft market can be divided into two types based on caliber size: Small Caliber and Large Caliber. They are commonly used in coronary artery bypass surgeries and peripheral arterial disease treatments. On the other hand, Large Caliber ePTFE Vascular Grafts have a diameter larger than 6 mm and are primarily used in procedures such as aortoiliac and infrainguinal reconstructions. Both types play a crucial role in addressing different vascular conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
