<p><p>TTL (Transistor-Transistor Logic) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) are types of Oscillators used in the electronic market. TTL oscillators utilize bipolar transistors, offering high speed and power consumption but lower noise performance. On the other hand, CMOS oscillators use MOSFET transistors, which provide lower power consumption and better noise performance, albeit at a slower speed. Both types cater to different market needs, with TTL being suitable for speed-critical applications, while CMOS is favored for lower power and noise-sensitive applications.</p></p>
