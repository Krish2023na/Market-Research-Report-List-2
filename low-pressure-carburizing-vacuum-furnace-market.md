<p><p>The low-pressure carburizing vacuum furnace market includes three types: single-chamber furnaces, double-chamber furnaces, and multi-chamber furnaces. Single-chamber furnaces consist of one chamber where the carburizing process takes place. Double-chamber furnaces have separate chambers for carburizing and quenching, allowing for a continuous production flow. Multi-chamber furnaces have multiple chambers that can be independently controlled, enabling simultaneous carburizing and quenching of different parts. These different types cater to various production needs and offer flexibility in the carburization process, depending on the desired outcomes.</p></p>
