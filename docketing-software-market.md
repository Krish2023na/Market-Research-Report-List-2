<p><strong>The Docketing Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Docketing software is available in two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based docketing software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and remote accessibility, allowing users to access their dockets from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises docketing software is installed and operated on local computers or servers within an organization's physical infrastructure. It provides data security and control, as the software and data reside on internal servers, but lacks the convenience and accessibility of cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
