<p><p>A luggage conveyor system is a mechanized system used in airports and hotels to transport passenger luggage from one point to another. These systems are designed to save time and minimize the manual effort required to transport luggage, reducing the risk of delays and improving overall passenger satisfaction.</p><p>The luggage conveyor system market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing number of air travelers worldwide and the need for a more efficient luggage handling process at airports. Additionally, the integration of smart sensors and artificial intelligence in conveyor systems allows for predictive maintenance, enhancing system reliability and minimizing downtime.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the implementation of self-service baggage drop-off systems, which enable passengers to check-in their luggage independently.
