<p><p>The Brachytherapy Catheter market offers two main types of catheters: Single Lumen Balloon Catheters (SLB) and Multi Lumen Balloon Catheters (MLB). SLB catheters have a single channel through which the radioactive source is delivered to the desired area. On the other hand, MLB catheters have multiple channels for delivering the radioactive source, allowing for more precise placement and control of the radiation dose. Both types of catheters play a crucial role in brachytherapy, a medical procedure that involves the precise placement of radiation sources directly at the site of a tumor.</p></p>
