<p><p>Automatic webbing cutting machines are categorized into two types: light webbing and heavy webbing. Light webbing cutting machines are specifically designed to cut thin and lightweight webbing materials. They are typically used in industries or applications where delicate and fine webbing products are involved, such as the fashion and textile industry. On the other hand, heavy webbing cutting machines are capable of cutting thick and heavy-duty webbing materials, which are commonly used in industries like automotive, construction, and cargo handling. These machines enable precise and efficient cutting according to the specific requirements of each webbing type.</p></p>
