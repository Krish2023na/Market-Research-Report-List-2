<p><p>The flatbed die cutting machines market comprises two main types of machines: automatic die cutting machines and semi-automatic die cutting machines. Automatic die cutting machines are fully automated and require little to no human intervention during the die cutting process. They are designed for high-volume production and offer faster operation and higher efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automatic die cutting machines require some manual input from operators during the die cutting process. They are suitable for smaller production volumes and offer a more cost-effective option for businesses with lower demands.</p></p>
