<p><p>A bridge crane, also known as an overhead crane, is a type of crane with the ability to move material horizontally and vertically within a confined space. Bridge cranes are commonly used in various industries, such as manufacturing, construction, and logistics, to lift and transport heavy loads with precision and efficiency.</p><p>The bridge crane market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 12% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Bridge Crane market consists of various types, including Single-Girder Bridge Cranes, Double-Girder Bridge Cranes, and other variations. Single-Girder Bridge Cranes have a single beam that runs along the length of the structure, with the hoist and trolley running beneath it. They are cost-effective and commonly used in light to moderate lifting applications. Double-Girder Bridge Cranes feature two beams for added durability and stability, suitable for heavy lifting tasks. Other market variations may include unique designs, configurations, or specialized features catering to specific industry requirements.
