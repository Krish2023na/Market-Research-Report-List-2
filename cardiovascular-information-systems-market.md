<p><p>Cardiovascular Information Systems (CVIS) market comprises three main types. On-Site CVIS involves hosting the system infrastructure and data on-premises within a healthcare facility or hospital network. Web-Based CVIS operates through a secure internet connection, allowing access from any location with internet connectivity. Cloud-Based CVIS employs remote servers to store and manage data, delivering access via the internet. These market types offer healthcare providers varying degrees of control, accessibility, and scalability in managing cardiovascular patient information and imaging data.</p></p>
