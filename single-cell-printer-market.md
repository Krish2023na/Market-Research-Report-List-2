<p><p>The single-cell printer market includes two main types: benchtop single-cell printers and handheld single-cell printers. These printers are larger in size and are capable of printing multiple cells at once, offering greater efficiency for high-throughput applications. On the other hand, handheld single-cell printers are portable and designed for on-the-go use.
