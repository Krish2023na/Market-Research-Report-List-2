<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Optical Cable</li><li>Multimode Optical Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre optic cables market is divided into two main types: single-mode optical cable and multimode optical cable. Single-mode optical cables are designed to transmit a single ray of light, making them suitable for long-distance communication and high bandwidth applications. Multimode optical cables, on the other hand, transmit multiple rays of light simultaneously, making them suitable for short-distance applications. Both types are used extensively in various industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, manufacturing, and data centers to provide efficient and reliable communication networks.</p></p>
