<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) Injector is a device that allows network switches to transmit both power and data through Ethernet cables. The injector injects electrical power into the Ethernet cable, which powers the connected device at the other end.</p><p>The PoE injector market is experiencing significant growth due to the rising adoption of PoE technology across various industries. The increasing demand for IP cameras, VoIP phones, and wireless access points is driving the market growth. PoE injectors are widely used in security applications, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and retail sectors.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing deployment of IoT devices and the growing need for power-efficient solutions are fueling the demand for PoE injectors. The ability to provide power and data over a single cable makes them highly desirable in smart building automation, industrial automation, and smart city applications.</p><p>Additionally, the adoption of Power over Ethernet Lighting (PoE Lighting) is expected to contribute to market growth.
