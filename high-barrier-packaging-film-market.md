<p><p>High barrier packaging films are used to provide protection to products against moisture, oxygen, light, and other external factors that can deteriorate their quality. Metallized films have a thin layer of metal vapor deposited on their surface, providing excellent barrier properties. Organic coating films are made up of organic materials that offer a high barrier to gases and water vapor. Inorganic oxide coating films consist of inorganic oxide compounds applied as a thin layer, providing superior barrier properties and high transparency. These films are widely used in various industries for packaging sensitive products.</p></p>
