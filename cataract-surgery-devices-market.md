<p><p>The cataract surgery devices market consists of three major types: Intraocular Lens (IOL), Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device (OVD), and Phacoemulsification Equipment. Intraocular Lens is a clear artificial lens implanted in the eye to replace the natural lens after cataract removal. Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Device is a gel-like substance used to maintain space in the eye during surgery. Phacoemulsification Equipment is used to break up and remove the cloudy cataract lens from the eye. These devices are crucial in enabling successful cataract surgeries and improving patients' vision.</p></p>
