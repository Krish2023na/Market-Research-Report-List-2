<p><ul><li>Regular Wearable Patch</li><li>Connected Wearable Patch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The regular wearable patch market refers to a category of patches that can be worn on the body, typically on the skin, to administer medication or monitor health conditions. These patches are self-contained and do not require external connectivity or integration with other devices to function. On the other hand, the connected wearable patch market encompasses patches that are capable of wirelessly connecting to other devices or networks, allowing for real-time monitoring of health data and remote communication with healthcare professionals. These patches offer more advanced features and integration options for a more comprehensive health monitoring experience.</p></p>
