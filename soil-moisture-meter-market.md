<p><p>The Soil Moisture Meter market consists of two main types: Pin Type and Catheter Type. Pin Type meters use metallic probes that are inserted into the soil to measure the moisture content. These types are often more affordable and suitable for small-scale applications. On the other hand, Catheter Type meters feature a catheter-like probe that is inserted into the soil to measure moisture. These types are typically more accurate and expensive, making them suitable for large-scale agricultural and research purposes. Both types provide valuable information about soil moisture levels, helping farmers optimize irrigation and improve plant growth.</p></p>
