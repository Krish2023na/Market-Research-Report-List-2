<p><strong>The Manual Glue Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Melt Glue Guns</li><li>Cool Melt Glue Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual glue guns market consists of two main types: hot melt glue guns and cool melt glue guns. Hot melt glue guns use a heated nozzle to melt the adhesive, which then cools down and solidifies upon application. They are commonly used in industries like construction and woodworking. On the other hand, cool melt glue guns use adhesives that do not require heating and can be directly applied onto surfaces. They are often preferred for delicate materials like fabrics and crafts. These glue guns cater to different needs and industries, offering versatility in adhesive applications.</p></p>
