<p><p>Micro volume pipettors are essential laboratory instruments used for accurate and precise handling of small liquid volumes. Single channel pipettors allow for the transfer of tiny volumes in a single sample at a time, making them suitable for applications requiring single transfers. On the other hand, multi-channel pipettors enable simultaneous handling of multiple samples, increasing efficiency and productivity. They are commonly used when multiple transfers need to be conducted simultaneously, saving time and effort. Both types of pipettors play crucial roles in laboratories, but their usage differs based on the specific requirements of the experiment or analysis.</p></p>
