<p><strong>The Pipetting Tip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipetting tip market can be divided into two types: non-filtered pipette tips and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are used in standard molecular biology experiments, providing accurate and precise measurements while allowing the transfer of liquid without any filtration. Filtered pipette tips, on the other hand, contain a built-in filter that prevents the transfer of aerosols, contaminants, or any potential for cross-contamination. These filtered tips are commonly used in PCR, genomics, and proteomics applications where maintaining a sterile environment is crucial to obtain reliable results.</p></p>
