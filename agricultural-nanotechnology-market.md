Soil improvement involves the use of nano-based materials to enhance soil fertility and nutrient absorption for improved crop growth. Water purification aims to develop nanofilters or nanosorbents for efficient removal of contaminants from water sources. Plant breeding utilizes nanotechnology for efficient gene transfer and manipulation. Nanoparticles production involves the manufacturing and utilization of nanoparticles in agriculture for various purposes.</p></p>
