<p><strong>The Wireless Portable Intercom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bluetooth Technology</li><li>Infrared Technology</li><li>Radio Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Portable Intercom market can be categorized into three types based on the underlying technology: Bluetooth, Infrared, and Radio. Bluetooth technology allows wireless communication between devices over short distances, typically within 30 feet. Infrared technology utilizes light signals to transmit data between devices that need to be in line-of-sight to establish a connection. Radio technology, on the other hand, uses radio waves for communication and offers a wider range of coverage, making it suitable for larger areas or outdoor settings.</p></p>
