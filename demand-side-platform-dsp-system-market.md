<p><p>A Demand Side Platform (DSP) System is a platform that allows advertisers to purchase digital ad space on various websites and apps. In the RTB (Real Time Bidding) market, advertisers bid on ad space in real time auctions, where the highest bidder gets the space. This is a common method for programmatic advertising. On the other hand, in the PPB (Programmatic Premium Buying) market, advertisers negotiate deals with publishers for specific ad space at a fixed price, typically for premium placements and highly targeted audiences.</p></p>
