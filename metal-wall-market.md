Rapid urbanization, infrastructure development, and the booming construction industry are fueling the market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the rising popularity of sustainable and energy-efficient building solutions is also boosting the metal wall market. Furthermore, advancements in manufacturing technologies have enabled the production of lightweight yet robust metal wall systems, making installation easier and more cost-effective.</p><p>Overall, the metal wall market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors like construction boom, sustainability concerns, and customization trends.
