<p><p>Airport display systems refer to the electronic systems used in airports to provide real-time information to passengers and other airport stakeholders. There are different types of airport display systems available. The Flight Information Display System (FIDS) displays flight schedules, departure/arrival times, gate information, and baggage claim details. The Ground Information Display System (GIDS) provides information about ground transportation facilities, such as taxi stands and bus stops. Other markets in this industry can include baggage handling systems, security management systems, and self-check-in kiosks. These systems play a vital role in enhancing passenger experience and improving airport operations efficiency.</p></p>
