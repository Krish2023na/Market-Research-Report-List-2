<p><p>EDC (Electronic Data Capture) System is a computerized system used to collect, store, and manage clinical research data in electronic format. It replaces traditional paper-based methods of data capture, allowing for more efficient and accurate collection, monitoring, and reporting of clinical trial data. EDC systems offer features such as real-time data validation, edit checks, audit trails, and role-based access control, ensuring data integrity and compliance with regulatory requirements.</p><p>The global EDC System market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of EDC systems in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries. The market is driven by the growing need for efficient data management in clinical trials, rising prevalence of chronic diseases, and technological advancements in cloud computing and mobile devices.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing focus on reducing the time and cost associated with clinical trials is further propelling the market growth. EDC systems streamline the data collection process, reduce manual errors, and improve overall efficiency, leading to cost savings for trial sponsors.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a rising demand for scalable and user-friendly EDC systems that can adapt to the evolving needs of clinical research.
