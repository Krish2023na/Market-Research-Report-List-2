<p><p>Industrial lanolin alcohol, also known as wool wax alcohol or lanolin fatty alcohol, is a natural fatty alcohol derived from lanolin. Lanolin is a waxy substance obtained from the wool of sheep during the wool processing, and industrial lanolin alcohol is obtained by saponification and further distillation of lanolin.</p><p>Industrial lanolin alcohol finds extensive applications in various industries including personal care, pharmaceuticals, coatings, lubricants, and textiles. In the personal care industry, it is used as an emulsifier, emollient, and moisturizing agent in products like creams, lotions, and lip balms. In the pharmaceutical industry, it is utilized in the formulation of ointments and topical drug delivery systems. It also serves as a key ingredient in the production of textile finishing agents, polishes, and leather treatments.</p><p>The market for industrial lanolin alcohol is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
