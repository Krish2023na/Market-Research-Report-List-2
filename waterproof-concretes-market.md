<p><ul><li>Waterproof Admixtures</li><li>Cement</li><li>Pozzolanic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof concretes are categorized into three main types: waterproof admixtures, cement, and pozzolanic market. Waterproof admixtures are additives that are mixed into concrete to enhance its resistance to water permeability. Cement refers to the binding material used in concrete production, and waterproof cement is designed to reduce water penetration. Lastly, the pozzolanic market includes materials like fly ash and silica fume which, when mixed with cement, improve concrete's waterproofing properties by reacting chemically with water to form additional compounds. All these types play a significant role in developing durable and water-resistant concrete structures.</p></p>
