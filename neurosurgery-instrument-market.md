<p><strong>The Neurosurgery Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intracranial Neurosurgery Instrument</li><li>Endonasal Neurosurgery Instrument</li><li>Spinal Neurosurgery Instrument</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurosurgery instrument market comprises various types of instruments used in different procedures. Intracranial neurosurgery instruments are specifically designed for surgeries inside the skull, addressing conditions such as brain tumors or aneurysms. Endonasal neurosurgery instruments are used for procedures involving the nasal cavity, treating conditions like pituitary tumors or sinus disorders. Spinal neurosurgery instruments are utilized for surgeries on the spine, including spinal cord tumors or herniated discs. The "Others" category encompasses instruments for neurosurgical procedures not covered by the aforementioned types, such as instruments for peripheral nerve surgeries.</p></p>
