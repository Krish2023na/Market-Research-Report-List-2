<p><p>An automatic wall mounted faucet is a type of faucet that is designed to automatically regulate the flow of water without the need for manual operation. These faucets are equipped with sensors that detect the presence of hands or objects, activating the water flow and shutting it off once no activity is detected. They are commonly used in public restrooms, commercial kitchens, and healthcare facilities due to their hygienic benefits and water-saving capabilities.</p><p>The global automatic wall mounted faucet market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period.
