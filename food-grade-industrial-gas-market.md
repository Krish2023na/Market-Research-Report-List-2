<p><p>Food grade industrial gas refers to gases that are used in the food and beverage industry for various applications including chilling, freezing, packaging, and preservation. Many food companies are opting for modified atmosphere packaging (MAP) that uses food-grade gases to extend the shelf life of products without the need for preservatives. This trend is driven by the growing consumer demand for eco-friendly and sustainable packaging options.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the growing popularity of cryogenic freezing technology.
