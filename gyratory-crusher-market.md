<p><p>Gyratory crusher market is segmented into two types: ordinary gyratory crusher and hydraulic gyratory crusher. Ordinary gyratory crushers rely on gravity for crushing materials, providing a high throughput and low operating costs. On the other hand, hydraulic gyratory crushers use hydraulic pressure to perform crushing tasks, offering features such as adjustable discharge sizes and automatic overload protection. Both types are widely used in mining, construction, and other industries to crush large rocks and ores efficiently.</p></p>
