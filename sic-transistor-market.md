<p><p>SiC (silicon carbide) transistor is a semiconductor device that utilizes silicon carbide as the base material for its construction. SiC transistors offer a wide range of advantages over traditional silicon-based transistors, such as higher breakdown voltage, faster switching speed, lower on-resistance, and better thermal conductivity. These characteristics make SiC transistors suitable for high-power applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, energy, and electronics.</p><p>The SiC transistor market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs) is a major driving factor for market growth.
