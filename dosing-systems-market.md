<p><p>Dosing systems are used in various industries to accurately control the dosing or dispensing of fluids or chemicals. Diaphragm dosing systems use a flexible membrane that moves back and forth to draw in and dispense fluids, providing precise and consistent dosing. On the other hand, piston dosing systems use a piston to precisely measure and deliver fluids. Both types of dosing systems offer high accuracy and reliability, making them suitable for applications such as water treatment, chemical processing, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
