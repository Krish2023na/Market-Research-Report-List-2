The rising demand for renewable energy sources, increasing investments in smart grid infrastructure, and the growing adoption of electric vehicles are the major factors driving market growth. Super capacitors are widely used to supplement the intermittent nature of renewable energy sources, improve the stability of power grids, and enhance the performance of electric vehicles by providing instant power during acceleration and regenerative braking.</p><p>Moreover, the advancements in super capacitor technology, such as the development of hybrid super capacitors and graphene-based super capacitors, are further driving market growth.
