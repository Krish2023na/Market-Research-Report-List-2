<p><p>Hotel PMS (Property Management System) market is characterized by three types: cloud-based, on-premise, and mobile. Cloud-based systems are web-based platforms that store data on remote servers, allowing access from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premise systems are installed locally on hotel servers, offering complete control and customizability but requiring maintenance. Mobile systems refer to PMS apps that allow hotel staff to manage operations using smartphones or tablets. Each type has its advantages and considerations, including cost, security, and flexibility, which hotels should consider when selecting a suitable PMS solution.</p></p>
