<p><p>Construction coatings refer to protective coatings used in the construction industry to enhance the durability, aesthetics, and performance of various surfaces. There are three main types of construction coatings: water-borne, solvent-borne, and powder coatings. Water-borne coatings are water-based and offer low toxicity, easy clean-up, and reduced emissions. Solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents and offer excellent adhesion and durability. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then heated, forming a hard finish. They offer excellent resistance to chipping, scratching, and corrosion, making them ideal for metal surfaces.</p></p>
