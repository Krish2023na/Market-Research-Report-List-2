<p><p>Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems and Decentralized Automated Dispensing Systems are two types of Automatic Pill Dispensers in the market. </p><p>Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems are typically used in large healthcare facilities, where medications are stored centrally and dispensed to individual patients through a network of automated dispensing cabinets. These systems provide controlled access, inventory management, and tracking of medications, enhancing medication safety and efficiency.</p><p>Decentralized Automated Dispensing Systems, on the other hand, are used in smaller healthcare settings like hospitals, clinics, or nursing homes. Medications are stored and dispensed at decentralized locations, such as nursing units or patient floors. These systems provide quick and convenient access to medications, reducing time spent on manual processes and improving medication adherence and patient care.</p></p>
