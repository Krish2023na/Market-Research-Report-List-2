<p><p>The 5G Equipment market consists of various types, such as Software-Defined Networking (SDN), Network Function Virtualization (NFV), Multi-Access Edge Computing (MEC), and Fiber and Cable (FC) market. SDN enables network management through software rather than physical infrastructure. NFV virtualizes network functions like firewalls or load balancers. MEC brings the cloud closer to the end-user, offering low-latency services. The FC market involves equipment like fiber optic cables that support high-speed data transmission. These technologies contribute to the development of efficient and advanced 5G networks for enhanced connectivity and communication.</p></p>
