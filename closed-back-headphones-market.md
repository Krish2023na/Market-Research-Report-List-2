<p><p>Closed-back headphones are available in two main market types: over-ear and on-ear. Over-ear headphones have larger ear cups that fully enclose the ears, providing better noise isolation and deeper bass. They are designed for immersive listening experiences, making them popular among audiophiles and professionals. On-ear headphones, on the other hand, have smaller ear cups that rest on the ears. They are more compact and portable, suitable for casual listening and everyday use. However, they may not offer the same level of noise isolation or bass response as over-ear headphones.</p></p>
