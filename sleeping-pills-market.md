These medications are commonly used to treat insomnia and other sleep-related disorders. Zolpidem is a sedative-hypnotic medication that is generally prescribed for short-term use. Adapin is a tricyclic antidepressant primarily used for treating insomnia associated with anxiety or depression.</p></p>
