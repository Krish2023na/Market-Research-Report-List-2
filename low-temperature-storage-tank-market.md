<p><p>A low temperature storage tank is a type of storage tank that is used for storing liquefied gases such as nitrogen, oxygen, argon, and liquefied natural gas (LNG). These tanks are designed to store the materials at extremely low temperatures, usually below -150°C (-238°F). Low temperature storage tanks are constructed using materials that can withstand these extreme temperatures, such as aluminum, stainless steel, and carbon steel.</p><p>The low temperature storage tank market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. The increasing demand for LNG as a cleaner fuel alternative is driving the market growth. LNG is used in various applications such as transportation, power generation, and industrial processes. The rising demand for LNG in emerging economies such as China and India is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the expansion of the cold chain industry is contributing to the growth of the low temperature storage tank market. With the increasing need for temperature-controlled storage and transportation of perishable goods, the demand for low temperature storage tanks is expected to rise.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the adoption of advanced technologies in low temperature storage tanks.
