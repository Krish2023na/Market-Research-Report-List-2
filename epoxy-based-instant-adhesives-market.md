<p><p>Epoxy-based instant adhesives are a type of adhesive that quickly forms strong bonds between various materials. The market is divided into two main types: cold cure adhesive and heat cure adhesive. Cold cure adhesive is designed to cure at room temperature, making it suitable for applications in which heat cannot be applied. On the other hand, heat cure adhesive requires the application of heat to initiate the curing process. This type of adhesive is commonly used in industries where high-strength bonding is required.</p></p>
