<p><ul><li>Special Type</li><li>Universal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Margarine market types can be broadly categorized into two types: special type and universal type. Special type margarine is typically made for specific purposes such as baking, cooking, or specific dietary needs. This type of margarine usually has a higher fat content and may contain additives or flavors suitable for targeted applications. On the other hand, universal type margarine is more versatile and can be used for various purposes like spreading on bread, cooking, or baking. It is often made with a lower fat content and may have a more neutral taste.</p></p>
