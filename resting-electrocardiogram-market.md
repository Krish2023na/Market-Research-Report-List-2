<p><p>A Resting Electrocardiogram (ECG) is a non-invasive medical test that records the electrical activity of the heart when a person is at rest. It involves placing electrodes on the chest, arms, and legs to measure the electrical signals generated by the heart. Resting ECGs are commonly used to diagnose various heart conditions such as arrhythmias, heart attacks, abnormal heart rhythms, and structural abnormalities.</p><p>The Resting Electrocardiogram Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The resting electrocardiogram (ECG) market types can be categorized into single lead, 3-6 lead, and 12-lead systems. Single lead ECG systems only require one electrode and provide a basic overview of the heart's electrical activity. 3-6 lead systems use multiple electrodes, allowing for a more detailed analysis and evaluation of different regions of the heart. 12-lead systems offer the highest level of detail, utilizing 10 electrodes placed strategically throughout the body to generate a comprehensive assessment of the heart's electrical signals.</p></p>
