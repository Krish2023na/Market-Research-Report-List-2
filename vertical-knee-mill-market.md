<p><p>A vertical knee mill is a type of milling machine where the spindle is vertically oriented to the table, allowing for the removal of material from the workpiece in a vertical direction. This setup offers several advantages, including the ability to perform tasks such as drilling and boring with precision and accuracy. Vertical knee mills are extensively used in various industries, such as manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and defense.</p><p>The global vertical knee mill market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
