<p><p>Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) are online learning platforms that offer free or low-cost courses to a large number of participants worldwide. MOOCs are divided into two market types: CMOOC (Connectivist MOOC) and XMOOC (eXtended MOOC). CMOOCs focus on learner collaboration and encourage participants to create their own learning paths through social interaction. XMOOCs follow a more traditional teaching approach and are instructor-centered, providing structured content and assessments. Both types serve different learner preferences: CMOOCs for those seeking collaborative and self-directed learning, and XMOOCs for those seeking a structured and instructor-guided learning experience.</p></p>
