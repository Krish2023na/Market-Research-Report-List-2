<p><p>Bin level indicators are used to measure the amount of material in a storage container. In the market, there are two popular types: infrared and ultrasound indicators. Infrared indicators use infrared beams to detect the material level, providing accurate and reliable readings. Ultrasound indicators, on the other hand, use high-frequency sound waves to measure the distance between the indicator and the material, effectively determining the level. Both types offer different features and benefits, catering to various industries and their specific needs in monitoring and managing inventory levels.</p></p>
