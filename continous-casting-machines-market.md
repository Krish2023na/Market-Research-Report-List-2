<p><p>Continuous casting machines are used in the metal industry to produce billets, blooms, and slabs. Curvilinear type machines have a curved mold, allowing for the continuous production of a wide range of metal products. This type is more flexible and efficient compared to other machines. On the other hand, radial type machines utilize a rotating mold to produce cylindrical or circular shapes. These machines are often used for producing seamless pipes and tubes. Both types of continuous casting machines offer different advantages and suitability for specific metal casting requirements.</p></p>
