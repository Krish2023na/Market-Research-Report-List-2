<p><strong>The Lightning Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lightning Protector market consists of three main types: Voltage Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Combination Type. The Voltage Switch Type protects against lightning surges by diverting excessive voltage away from sensitive equipment. The Pressure Limiting Type, on the other hand, limits the surge by regulating the pressure levels to a safe threshold. Finally, the Combination Type incorporates features from both types, providing a comprehensive protection solution against lightning strikes. Each type offers unique advantages based on specific needs and requirements of the users.</p></p>
