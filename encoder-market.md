<p><p>Encoder market can be broadly categorized into two types: rotary encoder and linear encoder. A rotary encoder measures the angular position of an object by converting it into electrical signals, commonly used in industrial applications like robotics and machine tools. On the other hand, a linear encoder detects linear displacement of an object and converts it into electrical pulses, commonly used in applications like coordinate measuring machines and CNC machines. Both types of encoders play a crucial role in providing accurate position feedback and enable precise motion control in various industries.</p></p>
