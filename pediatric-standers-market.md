<p><p>Pediatric standers are assistive devices used by children with physical disabilities to support them in an upright position. There are several types of pediatric standers available in the market. Mobile standers are equipped with wheels for easy mobility. Supine standers position the child in a lying position and offer support for activities. Prone standers position the child face-down for spinal alignment. Upright standers provide vertical support for standing. Three-way standers offer flexibility by allowing the child to switch between supine, prone, and upright positions. These standers enhance postural control and help promote effective functional movements in children with disabilities.</p></p>
