<p><p>Tabletop nebulizers are medical devices used to administer medication as a fine mist for inhalation. Compressor nebulizers, also known as jet nebulizers, use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist, making them efficient and commonly used for treating respiratory conditions. Non-compressor nebulizers, such as ultrasonic nebulizers or mesh nebulizers, use high-frequency sound waves or vibrating mesh to transform liquid medication into a mist. These types of nebulizers are generally quieter, portable, and ideal for use during travel or in situations where bulky equipment is not suitable.</p></p>
