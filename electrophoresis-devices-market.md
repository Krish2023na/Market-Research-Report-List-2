<p><p>Electrophoresis devices are utilized in different scientific research areas for separating and analyzing molecules. Gel Electrophoresis Systems utilize gel matrices to separate charged molecules based on size and charge. They are widely used in molecular biology, biochemistry, and forensic analysis. On the other hand, Capillary Electrophoresis Systems employ capillaries and an electric field to separate molecules. This technique offers higher resolution and flexibility, making it suitable for genomics, proteomics, and pharmaceutical research. Both systems are vital tools for studying DNA, proteins, and other biomolecules in various scientific disciplines.</p></p>
