<p><p>An indoor power wheelchair is a mobility device designed to assist individuals with limited or no walking abilities in navigating indoor spaces. Firstly, the rising prevalence of mobility impairing conditions, such as spinal cord injuries, muscular dystrophy, and cerebral palsy, is driving the demand for indoor power wheelchairs. Additionally, the increasing geriatric population, who often experience reduced mobility, is contributing to market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology are playing a crucial role in the development of indoor power wheelchairs. Manufacturers are incorporating features like adjustable seating positions, obstacle detection sensors, and intuitive control systems to enhance the user experience.</p><p>The market is witnessing a surge in demand for lightweight and compact power wheelchairs that can easily maneuver through narrow spaces and tight corners. Additionally, there is a growing trend towards customizable and aesthetically appealing designs to address the unique needs and preferences of users.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has also had an impact on the market, with a heightened focus on the importance of home healthcare solutions.
