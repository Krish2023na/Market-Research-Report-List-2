<p><p>The Transmission Control Units (TCUs) market encompasses two main types: Variable Transmission Control Units (V-TCUs) and Continuously Variable Transmission Control Units (CVT-TCUs). V-TCUs regulate the shifting of gears in vehicles with traditional automatic transmissions, adjusting the gear ratios according to the vehicle's speed and load demands. On the other hand, CVT-TCUs are specifically designed for vehicles equipped with Continuously Variable Transmissions (CVTs), which offer infinite gear ratios. CVT-TCUs ensure the smooth and efficient operation of these transmissions by controlling the pulleys and belts that vary the gear ratios continuously.</p></p>
