<p><p>Infrared thermometers are classified into three main types: Ear, Forehead, and Multifunction. Ear thermometers are specifically designed to measure the temperature inside the ear canal accurately. Forehead thermometers use infrared technology to measure the temperature from the forehead. Multifunction thermometers are versatile devices that can be used to measure temperature in various ways, such as ear, forehead, or even surface temperature. These thermometers are commonly used in medical settings, as they provide quick and non-invasive temperature readings without the need for physical contact.</p></p>
