<p><p>Mini aroma diffusers are available in two main market types: heating and wind blowing. Heating diffusers work by gently warming a tray or dish containing essential oils, causing them to release their fragrance into the air. These diffusers are often compact and portable, making them suitable for smaller spaces. On the other hand, wind blowing diffusers use a fan or blower to disperse the aroma throughout the room. They can cover larger areas and provide a more immediate fragrance experience. Both types offer convenient and efficient ways to enjoy the benefits of aromatherapy in a mini-sized device.</p></p>
