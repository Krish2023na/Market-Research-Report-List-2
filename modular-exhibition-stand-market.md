Compared to traditional custom-built stands, modular stands offer a more budget-friendly option. They eliminate the need for expensive construction materials and can be easily transported, reducing logistical costs.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements are also contributing to the growth of the market. The integration of digital screens, interactive touch panels, and lighting systems into modular exhibition stands creates an engaging and immersive experience for attendees.</p><p>In terms of market trends, sustainability and eco-friendly practices are gaining importance in the exhibition industry. Modular stands are known for their reusability and recyclability, making them an environmentally friendly option.</p><p>In conclusion, the modular exhibition stand market has a positive growth outlook due to the increasing demand from companies looking for flexible, cost-effective, and visually appealing display solutions.
