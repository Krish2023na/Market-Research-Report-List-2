<p><p>The ice blasting machines market consists of two main types: dry ice blasting machines and wet ice blasting machines. Dry ice blasting machines use solid CO2 pellets as the blasting medium, which convert directly from a solid to a gas upon impact, leaving no residue behind. Wet ice blasting machines, on the other hand, use crushed ice or ice cubes mixed with water as the blasting medium. Both types of machines are used for a variety of cleaning and surface preparation applications.</p></p>
