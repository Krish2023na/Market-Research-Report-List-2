<p><p>The robotic dispensing system market comprises two main types: ordinary dispensing systems and precision dispensing systems. An ordinary dispensing system is a robotic system that dispenses predetermined quantities of materials, such as liquids or powders, in a less precise manner. It is suitable for applications where accuracy is not crucial. On the other hand, precision dispensing systems are designed to dispense materials in highly precise quantities and are often used in industries where precision and accuracy are essential, such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, or aerospace.</p></p>
