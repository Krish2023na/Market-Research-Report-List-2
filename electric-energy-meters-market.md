<p><p>Electric energy meters are devices used to measure and monitor the consumption of electrical energy. They can be categorized into two types: single-phase electric energy meters and three-phase electric energy meters. Single-phase meters are designed to measure the energy consumption of single-phase electrical systems, typically found in residential buildings. Three-phase meters, on the other hand, are used to measure the energy consumption in larger commercial and industrial settings, where three-phase electrical systems are implemented. These meters provide accurate readings and enable efficient energy management in respective settings.</p></p>
