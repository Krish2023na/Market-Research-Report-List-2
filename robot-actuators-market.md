<p><ul><li>Linear Robot Actuators</li><li>Rotary Robot Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robot actuators are essential components that enable robots to perform physical movements. Linear robot actuators are designed to provide linear motion and are commonly used in applications requiring precise positioning such as assembly lines. They typically consist of a motor, gearing mechanism, and a rod that extends and retracts. On the other hand, rotary robot actuators enable rotational motion and are vital for tasks that involve turning or spinning, such as robotic arms or grippers. They usually comprise a motor and a mechanism to convert rotational motion into mechanical action.</p></p>
