<p><p>The Hyperhidrosis Treatment Market includes various types of treatments to manage excessive sweating. Medications involve using antiperspirants, oral drugs, and botulinum toxin injections to reduce sweating. Surgical treatments include sympathectomy, a procedure to remove or cut the nerves responsible for excessive sweating. Physiotherapy involves therapies like iontophoresis, which uses electrical currents to reduce sweat. Other treatments may include psychotherapy or acupuncture. These different types of treatments aim to provide relief to individuals suffering from hyperhidrosis by targeting the underlying causes of excessive sweating.</p></p>
