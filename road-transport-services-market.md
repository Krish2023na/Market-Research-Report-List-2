<p><p>Road Transport Services market types can be categorized into full loads and part loads. Full loads refer to shipments that take up the entire capacity of a truck, while part loads involve shipments that do not require the entire space available in a truck. Full load transportation is typically used for larger and heavier shipments that require dedicated transportation, while part load transportation is more cost-effective for smaller shipments that can be consolidated with other shipments to maximize the truck's capacity. Both types cater to the varying needs and sizes of businesses' transportation requirements.</p></p>
