<p><p>The 3D Image Sensors market is categorized into three main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor), and Others. CCD sensors convert light into electronic signals using a series of capacitors, offering high-quality images but consuming more power. CMOS sensors convert light into electrical signals using tiny photodiodes, delivering lower power consumption, cost-effective production, and better integration. The Other category includes sensor technologies like InGaAs and PMD, which provide different advantages such as enhanced sensitivity to certain wavelengths or faster acquisition speeds.</p></p>
