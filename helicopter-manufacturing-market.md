<p><p>Helicopter manufacturing market types can be categorized into three main segments: Single Rotor Helicopter, Twin-Rotor Helicopter, and Multi-Rotor Helicopter markets. Single Rotor Helicopters, also known as conventional helicopters, are the most common type and consist of one main rotor and one tail rotor. Twin-Rotor Helicopters, such as the tandem rotor or coaxial rotor design, have two main rotors spinning in opposite directions, offering increased stability and lifting capacity. Multi-Rotor Helicopters, like quadcopters or octocopters, utilize multiple rotors to achieve vertical take-off and landing capabilities. These different market types cater to various applications and customer requirements in the helicopter industry.</p></p>
