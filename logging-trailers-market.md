<p><ul><li>Anti-Lock Brakes (ABS or EBS)</li><li>Load Proportioning Brake System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Logging trailers are used to transport logs from forests to sawmills. The market types for these trailers include those equipped with Anti-Lock Brakes (ABS) or Electronic Braking Systems (EBS). ABS assists in preventing wheel lock-up during braking, reducing the risk of skidding. EBS further enhances braking efficiency by electronically controlling the brake force on each wheel individually. Load Proportioning Brake System refers to a mechanism that adjusts the brake force according to the weight distribution of the load, ensuring balanced and safe braking even when the load is unevenly distributed.</p></p>
