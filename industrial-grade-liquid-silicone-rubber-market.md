<p><p>The industrial grade liquid silicone rubber market can be categorized into three types based on their viscosity: low viscosity, medium viscosity, and high viscosity. Low viscosity liquid silicone rubber has a thin consistency, making it suitable for applications requiring precise molding and intricate designs. Medium viscosity liquid silicone rubber strikes a balance between flowability and stability, making it commonly used in general-purpose molding. High viscosity liquid silicone rubber has a thick consistency, making it ideal for applications that require high dimensional stability and heat resistance.</p></p>
