One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for these lifts in the construction and infrastructure sectors. Growing investment in construction activities, particularly in emerging economies, has created a significant demand for equipment that can facilitate work at height efficiently and safely. The rising emphasis on worker safety regulations and the need for efficient and cost-effective equipment have also fueled the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in trailer mounted boom lifts have further contributed to the market growth. Many manufacturers are incorporating advanced features such as automated controls and telematics systems to enhance the efficiency and safety of these lifts.
