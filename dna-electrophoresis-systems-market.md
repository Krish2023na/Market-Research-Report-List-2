<p><strong>The DNA Electrophoresis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li><li>Vertical Gel Electrophoresis Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA electrophoresis systems are used in molecular biology laboratories to separate and analyze DNA fragments. Horizontal gel electrophoresis systems involve placing DNA samples in wells at one end of a gel and applying an electric current to separate the fragments based on their size. Vertical gel electrophoresis systems involve stacking the gel vertically and applying the current from top to bottom. Both systems offer advantages and disadvantages, and their choice depends on the specific requirements of the experiment.</p></p>
