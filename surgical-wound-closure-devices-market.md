<p><ul><li>Closure Strips</li><li>Tissue Adhesive</li><li>Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Wound Closure Devices market consists of three main types: Closure Strips, Tissue Adhesive, and Sutures. Closure Strips are small adhesive strips that are applied to hold the edges of a wound together. Tissue Adhesive, also known as surgical glue, is a substance applied to the wound to bond the tissue together. Sutures are traditional stitches made with threads that are used to close the wound. All three devices are commonly used in surgical procedures to ensure proper wound healing and closure.</p></p>
