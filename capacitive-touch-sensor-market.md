<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Sensing</li><li>Projected Capacitive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive touch sensor market consists of two main types: surface capacitive sensing and projected capacitive sensing. Surface capacitive sensing technology uses a thin layer of conductive material overlaid on a screen, which detects touch input through changes in its electrical field. On the other hand, projected capacitive sensing employs a grid of micro-fine wires embedded within the screen to detect touch input, providing increased accuracy and multi-touch capabilities. Both technologies are widely used in various devices such as smartphones, tablets, kiosks, and automotive touchscreens.</p></p>
