<p><p>Lighting Control Dimming Panel is a device used to control the intensity of lighting in commercial and residential buildings. The market for these panels can be classified into two types: rack-mount and wall-mount. Rack-mount panels are installed in a rack enclosure and are suitable for large-scale lighting systems. They offer centralized control and are often found in theaters, stadiums, and convention centers.
