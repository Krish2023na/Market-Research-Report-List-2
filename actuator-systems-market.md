<p><p>Actuator Systems Market Types:</p><p>1. Electric actuators: These systems convert electrical energy into mechanical motion, utilizing an electric motor to actuate and control various mechanisms or devices.</p><p>2. Electromechanical actuators: These systems combine electrical and mechanical components, utilizing electric motors to convert electrical energy into linear or rotary motion.</p><p>3. Electrohydraulic actuators: These systems use hydraulic power in conjunction with electric control to actuate and control mechanisms, employing hydraulic cylinders and pumps.</p><p>4. Electrohydrostatic actuators: Similar to electrohydraulic actuators, these systems utilize hydraulic power, but with a closed-loop system that enables higher precision control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
