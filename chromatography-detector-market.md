<p><strong>The Chromatography detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Chromatography Detectors</li><li>Gas Chromatography Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatography detector market consists of two types: Liquid Chromatography Detectors and Gas Chromatography Detectors. Liquid Chromatography Detectors are used to analyze samples dissolved in liquid solvents. They detect the separated compounds based on their optical properties, such as UV-Vis absorption or fluorescence, or on their mass-to-charge ratio using mass spectrometry. Gas Chromatography Detectors, on the other hand, are designed to analyze volatile compounds. They typically use thermal conductivity, flame ionization, electron capture, or mass spectrometry to detect and quantify the separated compounds. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in chromatography analysis for various industries, including pharmaceutical, environmental, and food and beverage.</p></p>
