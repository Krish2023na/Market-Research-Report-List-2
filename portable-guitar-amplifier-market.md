<p><p>Portable guitar amplifiers are divided into two main types: combo amplifiers and head amplifiers. Combo amplifiers are all-in-one units that combine the amplifier and speaker in a single enclosure, making them compact and portable. They are suitable for small gigs and practice sessions. On the other hand, head amplifiers are separate units consisting of just the amplifier, requiring an external speaker cabinet for sound output. Head amplifiers offer flexibility and customization options for professional musicians and larger performances. Both types cater to the needs of guitarists, but with different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
