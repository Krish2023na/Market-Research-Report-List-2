<p><p>Blood dialysis machines are medical devices utilized to filter and cleanse blood for patients suffering from kidney failure or other kidney-related conditions. The market is categorized into four types: hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, hemofiltration, and others. Hemodialysis involves filtering the blood outside the body using a machine, while peritoneal dialysis uses the patient's peritoneum as a filter. Hemofiltration employs a combination of both filtration and convection methods. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses alternative dialysis methods such as hemodiafiltration and continuous renal replacement therapy, offering different options to address patients' specific needs.</p></p>
