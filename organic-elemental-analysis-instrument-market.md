<p><p>The Organic Elemental Analysis Instrument market consists of three main types: GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography involves the separation of complex mixtures, such as organic compounds, using a stationary phase and a mobile phase. Frontal Chromatography is a technique for separating substances through a mobile phase that displaces different analytes. Adsorption-Desorption refers to the process of molecules being attached to or released from a solid surface. These three types of analysis instruments are commonly used in laboratories for the identification and quantification of organic elements.</p></p>
