<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The micro pump market is divided into two types: mechanical micro pump and non-mechanical micro pump. Mechanical micro pumps rely on mechanical components like gears or pistons to generate fluid flow, allowing them to be controlled and adjusted easily. On the other hand, non-mechanical micro pumps use methods like electroosmosis or microfluidics to move fluids without any moving parts. These pumps are often smaller, have lower power consumption, and provide more precise fluid control. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications such as medical devices, analytical instruments, or microfluidic systems.</p></p>
