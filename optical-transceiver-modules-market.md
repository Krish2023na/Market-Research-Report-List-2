SFP modules are commonly used for data networking, while SFP+ modules provide faster data transmission rates. QSFP/QSFP+ modules offer high-speed data transmission over multiple channels. XFP modules are used for 10 Gigabit Ethernet connections, while CFP/CXP modules support higher data rates.</p></p>
