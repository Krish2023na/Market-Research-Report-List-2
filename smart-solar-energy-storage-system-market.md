<p><p>The AC-coupled solar battery market refers to a type of smart solar energy storage system where the battery is connected to the AC side of the solar inverter. This allows the battery to store excess energy generated by the solar panels and supply power back to the grid during peak demand or when solar generation is low. On the other hand, the DC-coupled solar battery market refers to a system where the battery is connected directly to the DC side of the solar panels, increasing overall efficiency and enabling energy optimization for specific loads.</p></p>
