The Closed Oil Chiller market consists of oil chillers that have a closed-loop system, wherein the oil being cooled does not come into direct contact with the outside environment. On the other hand, the Open Type Oil Chiller market includes chillers where the oil being cooled is exposed to the atmosphere. These different types of oil chillers cater to varying industry requirements, offering distinct advantages and applications.</p></p>
