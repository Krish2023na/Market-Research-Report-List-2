<p><p>A magnetic hyperthermia device is a medical device that utilizes magnetic nanoparticles and an alternating magnetic field to generate localized heating in a specific area of the body. This localized heating is used for various therapeutic purposes, such as the treatment of cancerous tumors. The device works by injecting magnetic nanoparticles into the body, which are then targeted towards the site of interest. Once the nanoparticles are concentrated in the desired area, an alternating magnetic field is applied externally, causing the nanoparticles to generate heat and effectively destroying the targeted cells.</p><p>The magnetic hyperthermia device market has been experiencing steady growth due to the increasing prevalence of cancer and the growing demand for minimally invasive treatment options.
