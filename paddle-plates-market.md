<p><p>Paddle plate market types can be classified into four categories based on their bore designs. Concentric beveled bore paddle plates have a single bore positioned concentrically, while quadrant bore plates have four separate bores surrounding a central hub. Segmental bore plates consist of several segments that together form the bore, and eccentric bore plates have a bore that is intentionally offset from the center. Each of these types offers specific advantages and is suitable for different industrial applications.</p></p>
