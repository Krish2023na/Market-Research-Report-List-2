<p><strong>The Automotive Electrically Operated Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Pump</li><li>Separate Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electrically operated oil pump market includes two main types: integrated pump and separate pump. An integrated pump is incorporated within the engine system and directly driven by the engine's crankshaft. It provides lubrication oil to the engine's moving parts. On the other hand, a separate pump is typically installed outside the engine and is driven by an electric motor or another power source. It serves the oil supply needs of different vehicle components such as turbochargers, transmission systems, and other auxiliary systems in the vehicle.</p></p>
