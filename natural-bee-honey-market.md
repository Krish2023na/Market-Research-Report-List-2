<p><strong>The Natural Bee Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyfloral Honey</li><li>Monofloral Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural bee honey is categorized into two main market types: polyfloral honey and monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey is produced when bees collect nectar from various types of flowers, resulting in a mixed floral flavor and aroma. This type of honey offers a diverse range of nutritional benefits due to the variety of plants visited by bees. On the other hand, monofloral honey is derived from the nectar of a single plant species, such as clover or lavender. This honey possesses a distinct taste and unique medicinal properties associated with the specific plant it originates from.</p></p>
