<p><p>Effervescent tablets are a popular dosage form of medication that dissolve in water to create a carbonated solution. This effervescence not only provides a pleasant fizzy sensation but also aids in the quick dissolution and absorption of the medication.</p><p>Effervescent tablets offer several advantages over traditional tablets or pills. They are easy to swallow, especially for individuals who have difficulty swallowing, such as children or older adults. The effervescence also masks the taste of certain medications, making them more palatable. Additionally, these tablets are known for their fast-acting properties and are particularly useful for medications that require rapid absorption, such as pain relievers and antacids.</p><p>The global effervescent tablet market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years.
