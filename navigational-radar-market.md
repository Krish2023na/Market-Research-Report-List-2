<p><ul><li>X Band Radar</li><li>S Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The navigational radar market includes two major types: X Band Radar and S Band Radar. X Band Radar operates in the frequency range between 8 and 12 GHz, offering high resolution and accuracy for short-range maritime applications. This radar is known for its strong target detection capabilities even in adverse weather conditions. On the other hand, S Band Radar operates in the frequency range between 2 and 4 GHz, providing a longer range coverage compared to X Band Radar. It is commonly used for long-range surveillance and tracking applications in the maritime sector.</p></p>
