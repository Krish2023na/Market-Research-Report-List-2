<p><p>Medical transfer boards are devices used to aid in transferring patients from one surface to another. There are two types of transfer boards available in the market. Transfer boards with rollers have small wheels attached, allowing for smoother and easier movement of the patient. These boards are particularly helpful when transferring patients between beds or wheelchairs. On the other hand, transfer boards without rollers require manual sliding of the patient, typically used in situations where rolling is not feasible or necessary. Both types of boards serve the same purpose of easing patient transfers, with the difference lying in the presence or absence of rollers.</p></p>
