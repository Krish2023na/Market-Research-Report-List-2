<p><strong>The 3D Concrete Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion-based</li><li>Powder-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D concrete printing market is segmented into two types: extrusion-based and powder-based. In extrusion-based printing, a specially formulated concrete mixture is extruded through a nozzle in layers, allowing for precise shape formation. This method is widely used in construction projects due to its cost-effectiveness and versatility. On the other hand, powder-based printing involves the binding of powdered material using a chemical reaction or energy source. This technique offers increased complexity in design and produces highly detailed and intricate concrete structures, making it suitable for architectural and artistic applications.</p></p>
