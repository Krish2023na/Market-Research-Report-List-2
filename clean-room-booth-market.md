<p><strong>The Clean Room Booth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Movable</li><li>Unmovable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clean room booths are special enclosures used in industries to maintain a controlled environment, protecting products from contaminants. These booths can be categorized into two main types: movable and unmovable. Movable clean room booths are designed to be easily transported and relocated, providing flexibility and adaptability to different production areas. On the other hand, unmovable clean room booths are permanently installed within a facility and offer a fixed, dedicated space for manufacturing processes. Both types serve the purpose of creating a clean and controlled environment; however, they differ in terms of mobility and flexibility.</p></p>
