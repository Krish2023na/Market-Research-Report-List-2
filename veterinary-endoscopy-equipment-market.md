<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy equipment market consists of various types, including flexible endoscopy, rigid endoscopy, capsule endoscopy, and others. Flexible endoscopy involves the use of a flexible tube with a camera to examine the internal body parts of animals. Rigid endoscopy uses a rigid tube attached to a camera for the same purpose. Capsule endoscopy involves the ingestion of a small camera in the form of a pill that captures images of the gastrointestinal tract. The "others" category includes specialized endoscopic procedures and equipment used in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
