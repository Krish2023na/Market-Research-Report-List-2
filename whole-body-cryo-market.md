<p><strong>The Whole Body Cryo Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Nitrogen Therapy</li><li>Dry Ice Therapy</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Whole Body Cryo market can be classified into three types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Therapy. In Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, the body is exposed to extremely cold temperatures achieved by liquid nitrogen. Dry Ice Therapy involves the use of dry ice to create a cold environment for the body. Electric Therapy, on the other hand, utilizes an electrically cooled chamber to provide the cryotherapy treatment. These different types of cryotherapy offer similar benefits in terms of reducing inflammation, aiding in muscle recovery, and improving overall well-being.</p></p>
