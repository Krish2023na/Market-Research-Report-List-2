<p><p>Gas power generators are devices that convert the chemical energy from natural gas into electrical energy. These generators employ an internal combustion engine that uses natural gas as a fuel source to produce mechanical energy, which is then converted into electrical energy using an alternator. Gas power generators are widely used as a backup power source in the event of grid failures or in areas where grid access is limited. They find applications in various sectors including residential, industrial, commercial, and transport.</p><p>The gas power generators market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period.
